# IceIQ - AI Hockey Analysis

## Short Description (80 chars)
AI-powered game analysis, player highlights, and hockey training for teams.

## Long Description (Google Play)

**IceIQ transforms how hockey teams analyze games, develop players, and train smarter.**

Upload your game footage and let our AI automatically detect every player, measure ice time, and generate personalized highlight reels — in minutes, not hours.

---

### 🏒 GAME ANALYSIS
- **Automatic player detection** — AI identifies jersey numbers and tracks every player
- **Ice time & shifts** — Precise measurement of each player's time on ice, shift counts, and averages
- **Zone analysis** — See exactly where players spend time: offensive, neutral, and defensive zones
- **Highlight clips** — Auto-generated highlight reels for each player, instantly shareable
- **Team analysis** — Full roster statistics, line combinations, and zone coverage maps

### 📊 PLAYER DEVELOPMENT
- **Ice time diagrams** — Animated rink diagrams showing player movement patterns shift by shift
- **Zone heatmaps** — Visual representation of where players are most active on the ice
- **Shift timeline** — Color-coded game timeline showing zone distribution at a glance
- **Position filtering** — Filter by position (LW, RW, C, D, G) and shooting direction (L/R)
- **Age group analysis** — Support for U8 through adult leagues

### 🎬 VIDEO LESSONS
- **Draw & Share** — Pause any video, draw tactical diagrams directly on the frame, and share via KakaoTalk, email, or text message
- **Tactical diagrams** — Animated breakout, power play, and forecheck patterns
- **Lesson library** — Curated hockey training videos with difficulty ratings

### 📋 TEAM MANAGEMENT
- **Roster registration** — Register players with jersey number, position, age, and shooting direction
- **Home/Away team separation** — Automatic team classification using uniform color AI
- **Team tactical analysis** — Zone possession, breakout success rate, and faceoff win percentage
- **AI tactical recommendations** — Automated suggestions for gap control, line changes, and zone entry

### 👤 PLAYER PROFILE
- **Individual ice time** — Complete ice time history and trend analysis
- **Position heatmaps** — See where each player contributes most on the ice
- **Comparison tools** — Compare players within the team
