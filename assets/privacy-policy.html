IceIQ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

IceIQ ("we," "us," or "our") operates the IceIQ mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address — used for account creation and authentication via Firebase.
  • Display name / team name — provided by you during sign-up.

Video and Analysis Data

  • Hockey game videos you upload are processed on our servers (RunPod) to generate player highlights, zone analysis, and ice-time statistics.
  • Videos are stored temporarily for processing and are not shared with third parties.
  • Analysis results (jersey numbers, ice-time data, heat maps) are stored in your account.

Usage Data

  • Number of analyses performed per month (for subscription limit enforcement).
  • Last login timestamp.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's services.
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe.
  • To enforce plan limits and display personalized analytics.
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, payment receipts).

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore (Seoul region).
  • Videos are processed on RunPod GPU servers and deleted after analysis.
  • Payments are processed by Stripe — we never store full card numbers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and database. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • YouTube — Embedded lesson videos. Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Uploaded videos are deleted from our servers within 30 days of processing.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Youth players (U8–U18) use the App under adult supervision (coaches/parents).

7. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your data.
  • Deletion — Request account and data deletion.
  • Correction — Update incorrect information via the Profile screen.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@iceiq.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
